## 1. Services Provided

### One-Time Setup
Provider will complete the following as part of the initial project:

- Domain registration and DNS configuration
- Website design, development, and launch
- Initial site content creation and migration
- Integration with third-party services listed below

### Monthly Maintenance
Beginning on the first full month after site launch, Provider will maintain the site on an ongoing basis. Monthly maintenance includes:

- Site hosting on Cloudflare's infrastructure
- Security updates and platform updates as needed
- Up to **______ hours** of content updates, changes, or support per month
- Monitoring of site uptime and performance
- Integration maintenance for the following services:
  - **Buttondown** — newsletter management and subscriber integration
  - **GiveButter** — donation platform integration
  - **Give.mn** — Minnesota giving platform integration

### Excluded Services
The following are **not** covered under this agreement:

- Facebook page creation, posting, or ongoing management
- YouTube channel creation, posting, or ongoing management
- Any other social media platforms
- Paid advertising or SEO campaigns
- Third-party platform accounts not listed above

- Restore service as quickly as possible in the event of an unexpected outage

Provider is **not responsible** for downtime caused by Cloudflare infrastructure failures, third-party service outages, or events outside Provider's control.

## 5. Term & Cancellation

This agreement is **month-to-month** and begins on the effective date above.

Either party may cancel by providing **30 days written notice** (email is acceptable).

If Client's account is more than **30 days past due**, Provider may suspend site services until payment is received. Provider will provide notice before suspending service.

**Upon cancellation**, once the account is current, Provider will:
- Transfer the domain registration to Client or Client's designee
- Deliver a complete export of all site files and content
